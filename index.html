About Us

This is our story

My name is Matt and my Daughters name is Lacey and this will be our story.

Neither of us have any background in Astrophotography but I do have an ever growing interest and Lacey is also studying photography at school.

We will be starting out using my mobile phone for the photography to start with to help keep costs as low as possible and we will be working in my back garden. I live in a Bortle 7 area so light pollution is an issue but with a bit of practice in post processing this should hopefully not ruin the images we get.

My plan is to get bigger and better equipment as and when funds are available to do so. So content will improve overtime.
